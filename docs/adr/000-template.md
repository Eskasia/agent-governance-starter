# ADR-000: Template

## Status

Proposed / Accepted / Deprecated / Superseded

## Context

What is the issue that we're seeing that is motivating this decision or change?

## Decision

What is the change that we're proposing and/or doing?

## Consequences

What becomes easier or more difficult to do because of this change?

## Reevaluation Triggers

What concrete project, product, scale, deployment, or dependency change would justify revisiting this decision?

## Switching Cost

What would become expensive to migrate if this decision changes later?

## Adoption Gate

What evidence, test, document update, or approval is required before adopting a new technology that touches this decision?

## Alternatives Considered

What other options were considered and why were they rejected?
